Adoption Conditions

I live in a big house located in the center of Amsterdam. All my cats live there freely and share the daily life of my large family.

I give them a lot of my time, give them the love and care they need to be happy and balanced.

The kittens are in constant contact with the members of my family, children, dog. They leave the cattery perfectly socialized and not before the age of 14 weeks. They will then be properly weaned and will no longer need their mother. They will grow into adult cats that are comfortable in their heads and on their feet.

I am committed to providing you with a healthy kitten, conforming to the breed standard, perfectly socialized.

I expect in return that you can provide him with the love, care and nourishment he needs to thrive and be a happy cat. It is a commitment that you make over a period of 15 to 20 years.

All adoption requests will be considered individually.

The kittens are intended for companionship and will leave the cattery sterilized.

The day of their departure will be given:

  • Up-to-date vaccination book
  • Dewormers
  • Fleas
  • Certificate of good health established by the veterinarian of the cattery
  • Sales contract
  • Pedigree request

The price of the kittens is fixed according to the type and color

The price is not non-negotiable.

When booking, a request for a deposit of 30% of the price of the kitten will be requested. This amount will not be refundable in the event of withdrawal, regardless of the reason.

Delivery possible (to be discussed).



My breeding is located in the heart of Amsterdam. All my cats are above all my little companions in life. My goal is simple: to offer them a pleasant living environment, to provide them with the care necessary for their well-being and to share unique moments with them.
